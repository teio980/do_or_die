About Us

Do or Cry is a platform dedicated to helping you overcome procrastination and build better habits. Our mission is to provide personalized tools, insights, and guidance to make habit formation easier and more enjoyable.

We believe that small steps taken consistently lead to big changes. Whether you're a student, professional, or lifelong learner, our platform helps you track your progress, understand your habits, and stay motivated every day.

From learning resources and quizzes to productivity tools and AI study buddies, everything is designed to support your growth and help you achieve your goals.
